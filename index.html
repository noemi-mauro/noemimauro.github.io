Projects
  • NODES Project - Nord Ovest Digitale e Sostenibile - Industria del turismo e cultura. 2022-Now

    Role: representative for the Computer Science Department of the University of Torino and researcher.
    Fund: PNRR, M4C2.
    Description: Innovative knowledge transfer for the enhancement of tourism through new products, destinations, itineraries. In particular, my contribution will focus on the design and development of a recommender system in the field of cultural heritage and the related presentation of suggestions to users.

  • RevIs Project 2022-Now

    https://silentscapes.eu/

    Role: workpackage leader and researcher.
    Fund: PRIN: Progetti di Ricerca di Rilevante Interesse Nazionale – Bando 2020.
    Description: The project "Revisualizing Italian Silentscapes 1896-1922. Landscapes and Locations in Early Italian Cinema a Hundred Years Later" focuses on increasing the visibility of film heritage with the development of new forms of semantic data organisation and new digital databases (DB). Moreover, the project aims to develop an application with thematic searches for individual sections of the audiovisual product and to combine the access to geo-localised data of film heritage with metadata search.

  • CARES Project 2022-Now

    http://cares.di.unito.it/

    Role: researcher, research and development about recommender systems of Points of Interest for people with autism.
    Fund: Fondazione CRT.
    Description: The project "Inclusive Personalised Tourism Systems for Autism (CARES)" focuses on the needs and interests of people with autism to support them in their tourism exploration. In particular, the project aims to enable adults with autism to have an enjoyable tourist experience for them, their caregivers and family members.

  • Triangolazioni 2021

    https://www.triangolazioni.unito.it/

    Role: task leader of the design of the mobile guide, researcher.
    Fund: Fondi di Public Engagement dell'Università degli Studi di Torino.
    Description: The Triangolazioni project focuses on the idea of triangulation as a tool to reconstruct a network of interconnections between histories - ancient and recent - scientists, intellectuals, discoveries, progress, revolutions. The project involves the development of a web application to visualize the semantic interconnections between different Points of Interest within a geographic map.

  • OPENALPLAB - "Rete di laboratori per la valorizzazione della didattica outdoor sugli ecosistemi alpini e prealpini piemontesi" 2021

    https://www.openalplab.unito.it/

    Role: task leader of the design of the mobile guide, researcher.
    Fund: Fondazione CRT.
    Description: The OPENALPLAB project focuses on the areas near to the Istituto Mosso (Passo Salati, Monte Rosa) and the laboratorio didattico Geodidalab (Lago San Michele, Ivrea). These sites have an high number of visitors but they show some problems in the contents fruition caused by the diverse environmental ecosystems. The project aims in improving the data fruition and the public engagement in these areas by paying attention to the environmental protection. Specifically, the project aims to actively involve the population and the schools with the use of ICT technologies in order to share and improve the information about territories. Within OPENALPLAB, my research focuses on the development of an intelligent system useful to explore the territorial information through the use of interactive widgets that allow to search and filter the desired data.

  • OnToMap 2016-Now

    https://ontomap.ontomap.eu/

    Role: initially software developer; since 2016 researcher and developer; since 2019 researcher and project manager.
    Fund: MIMOSA (MultIModal Ontology-driven query system for the heterogeneous data of a SmArtcity) - Progetto di Ateneo, 2015-17.
    Description: The web application OnToMap - Mappe di Comunità 3.0 was developed with the aim of responding to citizenship emerging needs, being configured as an easy and quick tool to consult spatial data, create custom maps, report to local administrators critical issues or new proposals. Within the OnToMap project, my research focused on the development of methodologies for information exploration support and geographic data visualization.

  • PIUMA Project 2019-2022

    http://piuma.di.unito.it/

    Role: researcher, research and development about recommender systems of Points of Interest for people with autism.
    Fund: Compagnia di San Paolo Foundation.
    Description: The project Personalized Interactive Urban Maps for Autism (acronym PIUMA) aims to help people with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) move and live within cities by means of a new digital service that provides interactive personalized maps. Within the PIUMA project, my research focused on the development of a recommender system of Points of Interest for people with autism that takes into account users' sensory aversions and preferences in making suggestions.

  • co3project 2019-Now

    https://www.projectco3.eu/it/

    Role: research fellowship (assegno di ricerca) for the design of the CO3OTM component (https://ontomap.eu/co3) that has the following roles in the co3project: semantic data interoperability among platform applications, cross-application logging of user actions, data hub for the CO3 applications and user achievements management (for gamification).
    Fund: the European Community through co3project: co3Project: co-create – co-produce – co-manage (H2020-SC6-TRANSFORMATIONS-2018-2019-2020 - CO3 Grant Agreement 822615).
    Description: CO3, Digital Disruptive Technologies to Co-create, Co-produce and Co-manage Open Public Services along with Citizens, aims at assessing the benefits and risks of disruptive technologies, namely: blockchain, augmented reality, geolocated social network, liquid democracy tools and gamification, in the co-creation, co-production and co-management of public services with citizens as PAs partners. Within the co3project, I focused on the design of data structures and inferential models for the attribution of achievements (points, badges, etc.) for a gamification model.
